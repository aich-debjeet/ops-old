INTRODUCTION

We are an online platform and social network for the arts and entertainment industry. Our services may be used by creative performing & non-performing artists and organisations to network, co-create and explore opportunities to meet/advance their profession/career/ objectives.


This policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect information in relation to our website located at www.onepagespotlight.com or mobile services, off-site services like ad services and plug-ins, and any software provided on or in connection with One Page Spotlight services (collectively, the "Service"), and your choices about the collection and use of your information


By using our Service you understand and agree that we are providing a platform for you to post content, including videos, photos, audio, messages, comments and other materials ("User Content"), to the Service and to share User Content publicly. This means that other Users may search for, see, use, or share any of the content that you make publicly available through the Service, consistent with the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.


INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT

information that you provide us


When Signing Up & Using Paid Services
To register with us you provide us with your name, email address and/or mobile number, and a password. If you register for a paid Service, we ask you for payment (e.g., credit card) and billing information. If you were to buy or use a service on behalf of someone else, you’d also provide us their shipping details and contact information.


When You Update Your Profile
To update your profile, you may choose to provide us information such as age, gender, ethnicity, physique, body proportions, qualifications, work experience, skills, awards, achievements and contact information such as email address, telephone number, physical location, and any other information you provide us. Profile information helps you to get more from our Service, including helping potential collaborators, recruiters and opportunity providers to find you. If you use our mobile Service, you can choose to share your location data. It’s your choice whether to include sensitive information on your profile. Please do not post or add personal data to your profile that you would not want to be publicly available.


When You are Posting and Uploading
We collect personal data from you when you provide, post or upload it to our Services, such as when you create channels/spotfeeds that may consist of writings, compositions, photos, audio clips and/or videos that you make available. We also collect data when you create events, use our scheduler/calendar, fill out a form/survey, respond to a networking request, submit a resume and apply for or save jobs or send invitations. If you opt to import your address book, we receive your contacts (including contact information of your service provider(s) or app automatically added to your address book when you communicated with addresses or numbers not already in your list). If you sync your email or calendars with our Services, we will collect your “email header” and calendar meeting information (e.g. times, places, attendees and contacts).


When using messaging services
We collect information about you when you send, receive, or engage with our messaging service. For example, if you get a networking request, we track whether you have acted on it and will send you reminders. We also use automatic scanning technology on messages.


information from others
In Content or in the News
You and others may post content that includes information about you on our Services (as part of channels, feed updates and comments). Unless you opt-out, we may collect public information about you, such as profession-related news and accomplishments and make it available as part of our Services (e.g. suggestions for your profile, trending profiles/channels, or notifications of mentions in the news).


From Contact Lists and Calendar Information
We may receive personal data about you when others import or sync their address book or calendar with our Services, or send messages using our Services (including invites or networking requests). From Other Registered Users, Customers or Partners


We receive personal data about you when you use the services of our customers and partners, such as prospective employers. Online advertisers typically share information with the websites or apps where they run ads to measure and/or improve those ads. We also receive this information, which may include information like whether clicks on ads led to purchases or a list of criteria to use in targeting ads.


technical information when you use our services
Analytics Information
We use third-party analytics tools to help us measure traffic and usage trends for the Service. These tools collect information sent by your device or our Service, including the web pages you visit, add-ons, and other information that assists us in improving the Service. We collect and use this analytics information with analytics information from other Users so that it cannot reasonably be used to identify any individual User. We offer our users the option to opt-out of analytics tracking on the user settings page. We also respect “Do Not Track” browser settings. If you choose either of these options, we make best efforts not to send any data about your user behaviour, anonymized or otherwise, to any other third party service provider. Please be aware that there may be other services that you are using that may continue to send information to Google when you use the Site, even if you have asked us not to send information through our services.


Cookies & Similar Technologies
When you visit the Service, we may use cookies and similar technologies like pixels, web beacons, and local storage to collect information about how you use One Page Spotlight and provide features to you. We may ask advertisers or other partners to serve ads or services to your devices, which may use cookies or similar technologies placed by us or the third party. More information is available in our About Cookies section


Usage Log Files
Log file information is automatically reported by your browser each time you make a request to access (i.e., visit) a web page or app. It can also be provided when the content of the webpage or app is downloaded to your browser or device. When you use our Service, our servers automatically record certain log file information, including your web request, Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, referring / exit pages and URLs, number of clicks and how you interact with links on the Service, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed, and other such information. We may also collect similar information from emails sent to our Users which then help us track which emails are opened and which links are clicked by recipients. The information allows for more accurate reporting and improvement of the Service.


Device & Location Identifiers
When you use a mobile device like a tablet or phone to access our Service, we may access, collect, monitor, store on your device, and/or remotely store one or more "device identifiers." Device identifiers are small data files or similar data structures stored on or associated with your mobile device, which uniquely identify your mobile device. A device identifier may be data stored in connection with the device hardware, data stored in connection with the device's operating system or other software, or data sent to the device by One Page Spotlight. A device identifier may deliver information to us or to a third party partner about how you browse and use the Service and may help us or others provide reports or personalized content and ads. Most devices allow you to prevent location data from being sent to us and we honor your settings.


Metadata
Metadata is usually technical data that is associated with User Content. For example, Metadata can describe how, when and by whom a piece of User Content was collected and how that content is formatted. Users can add or may have Metadata added to their User Content including a hashtag (e.g., to mark keywords when you post a video), geotag (e.g., to mark your location to a photo), comments or other data. This makes your User Content more searchable by others and more interactive. If you geotag your photo or tag your photo using other's APIs then, your latitude and longitude will be stored with the photo and searchable (e.g., through a location or map feature) if your photo is made public by you in accordance with your privacy settings.

YOUR CHOICES ABOUT YOUR INFORMATION

you control your data

Access and change information in your profile at any time, you can choose whether your profile available to search engines, or choose whether others can find your One Page Spotlight account using your email address/contact number. You can login and update your privacy settings anytime. Unsubscribe from email communications from us by clicking on the "unsubscribe link" provided in such communications. You cannot opt out of Service-related communications (e.g., account verification, purchase and billing confirmations and reminders, changes/updates to features of the Service, technical and security notices).


your spotfeeds & spotscore

Create or control who can access each of your Spotfeeds/Channels. Public Spotfeeds/Channels are visible to all. For Public Spotfeeds/Channels, any members may choose to make the contents available for viewing and sharing to anyone else. If you choose to mark a Spotfeed/Channel as ‘Private’, they are visible to you by default and those specific members or your list of followers with whom you have shared the content. For Private Spotfeeds/Channels, members or followers with whom you have shared the Spotfeed/Channel will be unable to share the content with anyone else. However please remember that another member or follower may just take an image from the Spotfeed/Channel and email it to their friends. You have the choice of who can see your Spotscore. You may decide to keep the Spotscore private (viewable only to you) or provide limited access (to specific members and followers only) or make it public (viewable by all). For more information on Spotscore please click here.


account closure & data retention

If you choose to close your One Page Spotlight account or if your account is deactivated or terminated, your personal data will generally stop being visible to others on our Services within 24 hours. We will retain de-personalized information after your account has been closed. Information you have shared with others (e.g., through messages, likes, updates or group posts) will remain visible after you closed your account or deleted the information from your own profile or mailbox, and we do not control data that other members copied out of our Services. Your profile may continue to be displayed in the services of others (e.g., search engine results) until they refresh their cache.
