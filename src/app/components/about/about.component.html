About Us

Artists, performing and non-performing with great talent and abilities go unnoticed without opportunities to gain exposure, collaborate with other talents and resources to progress in their forms of arts. Existing art communities & organizations are challenged with market value of arts, available funds and are geographically isolated from potential new audience. Having experienced the ills and difficulties in the arts & entertainment industry, we have ventured to build an ecosystem for this industry that would enable individuals and organizations to thrive and establish themselves. We are committed to empower the creative performing & non-performing artists and organizations to network, create and advance. On a single platform we enable intercultural, intergenerational and cross industry collaborations both locally and internationally. Our website provides a toolkit and resources for aspiring students, professionals & businesses in the creative community to showcase talent and monetize creative practice. With deep knowledge of the audience persona, we have designed, categorized and curated feeds based on channels of personal interests to provide an immersive experience and a transcendental entertainment to viewers and critics. We strive to empower artists for they in turn can entertain the whole wide world!
