Product Features

Channel

Channels are like folders to organize and share multi media content based on your specific skills to ensure you reach the right audience with similar interests.

Spotfeed

Feeds designed, categorized & curated into multimedia channels of personal interests

Opportunity

Artists can collaborate on projects with multiple forms of talents across all industries. Companies can post jobs full time or part time. Charity Organisations can seek volunteers and performers for events.

Event

Users can create, invite, share & attend events which can be categorized into workshops, performances and movies that may be ticketed or offer free entry.

Community

An effective way to build connection and engagement, communities help in bringing together people of the same interests, kick up conversations around the central theme of the group, and keep things vibrant

Pedia

Curated content for all art forms, crafts & skills required in the arts industry for any one aspiring to learn and progress in their fields, careers or interests.
